## ğŸ¯ Transforming Robotics Development Through Virtualization

Welcome to Module 2 of the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics textbook. This module introduces **Digital Twin technology**, a revolutionary approach that creates virtual replicas of physical robotic systems. Digital twins enable comprehensive testing, validation, and optimization of robotic behaviors in a safe, controlled virtual environment before deployment to real hardware.

## ğŸ—ï¸ Core Applications in Humanoid Robotics

Digital twin technology has become particularly critical for humanoid robotics development due to the complexity and cost of physical humanoid platforms:

### ğŸ¤– **Humanoid Locomotion Simulation**
Testing walking, running, and balance algorithms in virtual physics engines before transferring to real robots.

### ğŸ‘ï¸ **Sensor Integration**
Simulating cameras, LIDAR, IMUs, and other sensors to validate perception algorithms in a controlled environment.

### ğŸ¤² **Manipulation Skills**
